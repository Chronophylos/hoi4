Combat Width

The effectiveness of a Combat Width depends on the Theatre but I'll ignore the differences. Fabricensis[Fabr2021] goes into more detail regarding these differences in his report on Combat Widths.

Furthermore he concludes,

10 widths are on paper the best divisions. However it is difficult to balance their stats, especially with support companies. For small countries however, it is very viable to build up a small military with many low width divisions.

27 seems to be the best rounded width in the medium range, 25 should be avoided.

If you can afford the industrial and supply cost, 41 to 45 is a high plateau, leading to some wiggle room.

All in all, the differences between different widths are quite small, so it can still be a good strategy to build specialized divisions, like 30 or 45 width tanks or 25 width mountaineers
